How do you apply these Mindfulness tips?

  • You can certainly move forward with these Mindfulness tips, but don't try to apply them all at the same time
  • Write down and carry the Mindfulness tips that suit you. Read them a few times a day so that they stay in your memory. If you are used to applying those Mindfulness tips you can try 1 or 2 new ones again.
  • Take it easy and see what works best for you. No hurries, No worries, Be Mindful!
